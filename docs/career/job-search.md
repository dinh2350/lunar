---

## Standing Out Tips

1. **Customize every application.** Reference the company's specific AI work.
2. **Lead with the project.** Lunar is more impressive than generic job experience for AI roles.
3. **Include metrics.** "93% accuracy", "100 days", "12 tools" ‚Äî concrete numbers stick.
4. **GitHub link in every application.** Make it easy to see your code.
5. **Follow up at 1 week.** Polite LinkedIn message to hiring manager or recruiter.
6. **Contribute to their open source.** A PR to their repo is the ultimate cover letter.
